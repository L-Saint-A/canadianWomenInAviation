What We Do

  • Conference

    The energy you pick up at a CWIA conference will fill your cup long after you leave. This event never fails to deliver big laughs, cozy chats, eye-opening insights, mentors and friends. Not only do you get an amazing stage program, the tours, the socials and the gala really make this event a 5-star experience.

  • Circles

    The place to find your next aviation bestie. This monthly social happens from coast to coast - because it's online. It's not just another meeting. It's a chance for small talk, deep chats and real connections. You might even run into an aviation celebrity. ;)

  • Approaching Finals

    For student pilots, by student pilots - Approaching Finals helps you soar through flight training. Show up and run into a friend, a big sis, a study buddy, or your future co-pilot, who gets what you're going through. School's always more fun when you have someone to sit within the cafeteria.

  • Calls

    We learn through stories. And our stories are often well-kept secrets with how hard they are to find. Enter CWIA Calls - your chance to hear great stories from the coolest aviation women. You'll laugh, you'll cry and come away from these calls inspired and energized.
